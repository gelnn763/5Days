Sample eviction notice · Chicago, IL
5-DAY NOTICE TO PAY RENT OR QUIT To: Maria Gonzalez 2847 W. Cermak Rd, Apt 3B Chicago, IL 60608 You are hereby notified that there is now due and owing from you to the undersigned landlord the sum of $1,150.00 for rent of the premises located at the above address for the period of October 1–31, 2024. You are further notified that payment of said sum must be made within FIVE (5) DAYS of service of this notice or you must quit and deliver up possession of said premises. Dated: October 3, 2024 R. Sterns Properties LLC
🔴
Notice type
5-Day Notice to Pay Rent or Quit
⏰
Deadline
October 9, 2024 — 6 days from now
⚠️
Potential defect detected
Notice not signed by individual agent as required by Chicago RLTO §5-12-130. This may be a valid defense — bring to legal aid immediately.
⚖️
Key right
Under Chicago RLTO, you have the right to pay rent in full within the 5-day period and the eviction proceeding must stop.
